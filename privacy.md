# IGDL Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 20, 2026

IGDL does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

- No account is required.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
- No data is sent to any server — IGDL has no backend.
- Everything you preserve in IGDL stays on your device and in your
  Photos library.
- IGDL requests Photos access solely to save and read the memories
  you explicitly preserve.

IGDL only accesses publicly available Instagram content that you
explicitly share into the app. IGDL is not affiliated with or endorsed
by Instagram or Meta Platforms, Inc.
